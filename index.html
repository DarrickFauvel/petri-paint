About the App

Petri Paint is the product of a Mintbean hackathon submission. The goal of the project is to build an app so that a user can move their mouse across the screen and draw free-form lines.

Instructions

  • Click on << Try the App >>.
  • Use the mouse to click and draw your favorite virus, bacterium, paramecium, etc., onto the petri dish.
  • Adjust the Tool Size by clicking the increase (+) and decrease (-) buttons.
  • Change the tool color by selecting a new color from the Palette.
  • To wipe the petri dish clean, click the Clear button.
